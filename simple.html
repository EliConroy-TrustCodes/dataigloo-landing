How it works

1. Ingest hot

New or frequently accessed data lives on production or cloud tiers to meet performance SLAs.

2. Detect inactivity

Policy thresholds (e.g., no reads for 90 days) flag candidates for tiering.

3. Tier to cold

Move inactive data to sovereign, air‑gapped vaults with WORM + integrity checks. Retrieve on demand.

Why cold data storage?

Prevent primary storage overload
Utilise economical cold media
Reduce total storage TCO
Simplify management via policy tiering
Meet compliance & governance efficiently
